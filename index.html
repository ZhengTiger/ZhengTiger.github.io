👤 ABOUT ME

I am a Ph.D. student at Huazhong Agricultural University (HZAU) in China. My research interests are bioinformatics data analysis and related software development. Specifically, my current research focuses on:

  1. single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNAseq) data analysis,
  2. Spatial transcriptomics (ST) data analysis,
  3. Neurobiology, particularly mapping neural circuits through high-throughput sequencing.

I am also interested in bioinformatics software development. More tools and projects I developed can be found in the Projects part.
